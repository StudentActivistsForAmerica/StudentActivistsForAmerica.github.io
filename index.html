Introductions

Wed Jan 25, 2017 by SAFA.

Hi everyone! We are SAFA, a political activism organization for Bay Area students. We focus on real issues that are present in today’s America, such as women’s health, racial discrimination, health care, poverty, the environment, and many more. We then take these issues and work to fix them, by holding fundraisers and donation drives, volunteering, protesting, and spreading awareness, usually working on one issue per month. The difference between our mission and that of other activism organizations is that we intend to engage students primarily and foster engagement in one’s community regardless of the individual causes that they work with specifically.
