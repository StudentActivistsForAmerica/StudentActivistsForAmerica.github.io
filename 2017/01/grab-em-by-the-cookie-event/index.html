Grab ‘em by the Cookie Event

Thu Jan 26, 2017 by SAFA. Categories: event / fundraiser .

Our first event is “Grab ‘em by the cookie”, a bake sale for Women’s Health, with the proceeds going to Planned Parenthood. As any even, we need lots of volunteers and help to accomplish this task. Even if you can’t volunteer, you can still ask someone else if they would like to help. We will be updating you all right here, on this channel, as more info about the bake sale as we get closer to the date. But without further ado, here is the info we have:

When: Thursday February 9th and Friday February 10th

Where: On 2/9/17, we will be holding the sale at the Nueva School Bay Meadows Campus. On 2/10/17, the sale will be held at the Hillsdale Mall.

What we Need: We need volunteers to make treats and other goods for the sale. Fill out the form to sign up to bring something: https://goo.gl/forms/Szp7UV3SSbBCvUki1

Thank you for volunteering for this event and we hope it will be amazing!
