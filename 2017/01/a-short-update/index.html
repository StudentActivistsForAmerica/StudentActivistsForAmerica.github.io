A Short Update

Mon Jan 30, 2017 by SAFA.

We are now 10 days away from the bake sale and we still have lots to do. One of the most important ways we need you to help is by baking for us. Right now, we have 5 people who have signed up to bake. Let’s try to get that to 10 people. If you are thinking about baking, please sign up. If you’re thinking about it but not sure if you can, write in a note telling us that.

Here is the sign up sheet, and remember, we can’t do this without you: https://goo.gl/forms/Szp7UV3SSbBCvUki1

Thanks, The SAFA Leadership
