Friday Update 1/27/17

Fri Jan 27, 2017 by Aidan. Categories: Friday Update .

Happy Friday SAFA Volunteers! Every Friday we will be releasing an update summing up the week, telling you our plans for the future, and detailing what you can do over the weekend. Recap of the Week There is a lot to go over this week, as we started it all on Tuesday. We organized SAFA and determined our debut event: the bake sale for Planned Parenthood. You can look at the previous couple of posts to see the details of that, but to summarize, we chose a date (2/9/17 and 2/10/17), locations (Nueva and Hillsdale Mall), created sign up sheets, and have started to flesh out the event even more.
